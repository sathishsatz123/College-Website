About Us

Academics

The college offers five undergraduate courses leading to the Degree of Bachelor of Engineering (B.E).

under graduate courses

  • B.E. - Mechanical Engineering
  • B.E. - Computer Science and Engineering
  • B.E. - Civil Engineering
  • B.E. - Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • B.Tech. - Information Technology

Though college boasts of Industry embedded education, the actual curriculum follows Anna University syllabus. The college has an advantage of shared resources with KGCAS Institution. The students are indeed presented with courses apart from the regular syllabus but with mediocre results.

facilities

KGiSL Institute of Technology

The college has classrooms, laboratories, workshops, library, Internet (24 Mbit/s) connectivity. The college has the modern e-Campus system for the students easy functioning. Teaching staffs are known to be very strict and the college is known to be the strictest in the City. Security personal in the campus are known to behave very rude towards students and visitors.
